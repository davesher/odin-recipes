Creamy Vanilla Frozen Yogurt

Description

A creamy and delicious frozen yogurt with a subtle hint of vanilla.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Greek yogurt
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. step

    2. Mix together the yogurt, sugar, and vanilla. Refrigerate the yogurt mixture for 45 minutes.

  2. step

    3. Pour the yogurt mixture into the bowl of an ice cream maker and freeze according to the manufacturer's directions.
